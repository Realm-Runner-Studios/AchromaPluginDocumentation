In Firebase, make sure that under "{}\+Remote Config"{} there\textquotesingle{}s a parameter with the name of the app + "{}\+Last\+Version"{} + platform. For example, if the bundle identifier on Android is com.\+realmrunnerstudios.\+achroma\+Adenture, a parameter must be third element of the bundle identifier (achroma\+Adventure) + "{}\+Last\+Version\+Android"{}. The result would be "{}achroma\+Adventure\+Last\+Version\+Android"{} For i\+OS, if the bundle identifier is "{}com.\+realmrunnerstudios.\+achroma-\/adventure"{}, the result would "{}achromadventure\+Last\+Versioni\+OS"{} (the hiphen is not accepted on Firebase)

To use the plugin, add {\ttfamily using \doxylink{namespace_achroma_a_p_i}{Achroma\+API};} to the head of your script.

This plugin has areas of usage depending on what you need todo\+:

{\ttfamily Achroma\+Instance\+Assets} for anything related to Assets like Characters, Locations, Objects and Actions.

{\ttfamily Achroma\+Instance\+Auth} for anything related to Sign In, Sign Out

{\ttfamily Achroma\+Instance\+Achievements} for anything related to user\textquotesingle{}s achievements

{\ttfamily Achroma\+Instance\+Database} for anything related to the user\textquotesingle{}s account and their data

{\ttfamily Achroma\+Instance\+Match} for anything related to Companion match

{\ttfamily Achroma\+Instance\+Shards} for anything related to Shards Animations

{\ttfamily Achroma\+Instance\+Story} for anything related to the Adventure Mode

{\ttfamily Achroma\+Instance\+UI} for anything related to UI elements standardized for \doxylink{namespace_achroma}{Achroma}
