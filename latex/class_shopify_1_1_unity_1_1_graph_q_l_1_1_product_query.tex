\doxysubsection{Detailed Description}
A product represents an individual item for sale in a \doxylink{namespace_shopify}{Shopify} store. Products are often physical, but they don\textquotesingle{}t have to be. For example, a digital download (such as a movie, music or ebook file) also qualifies as a product, as do services (such as equipment rental, work for hire, customization of another product or an extended warranty).
